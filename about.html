What do we do?

We aim to sharpen our readers' views of the world through giving the cold truth about the post-apocolyptic world, regardless of good or bad.

Why can't we write about something nice?

We write about what we believe is important. Right now, there is more bad than good in the world. We want to make sure that are readers are aware of this.

Why do you hate Mayor McDonough so much?

I discuss him in my latest article, but along with this I like some other people in Diamond City find his refusal to have kidnappings investigated very worrying. Who does he really work for?
